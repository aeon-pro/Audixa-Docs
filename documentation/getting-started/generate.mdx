You can generate high-quality speech directly from the web dashboard without writing any code.

1. Visit the [TTS Dashboard](https://audixa.ai/dashboard/tts).
2. Select Base Model
3. In the text box, enter a test script like:

```
Lily looked out the window and smiled. The morning sun painted golden stripes across her desk, and a new day of adventure had just begun.
```
3. Select a voice from the dropdown.
:::tip
Voices marked with ðŸ’Ž are not available on Base Plan
:::
4. Adjust the **voice speed** to your preference.
5. Click the **Generate** button.

Your audio will be ready in a few seconds, and you can listen or download it directly from the page.
